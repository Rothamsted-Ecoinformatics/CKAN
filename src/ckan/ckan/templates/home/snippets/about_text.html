This is a Data Repository of Rothamsted Research Built on Top of CKAN

This Service will allow the users to store, publish, search and download data. We follow the DataCite Schema for storing the metadata. DOI for the resources are generated within the CKAN and will be resolvable

The service provide a secure, robust and user friendly system to manage their data. This site will be open to the public and there by people outside the organization will be able to see or download the metadata or resources of a dataset unless it is specified to be a private dataset.

CKAN: https://ckan.org/
CKAN Showcases: https://ckan.org/showcase
Features overview: https://ckan.org/features/
